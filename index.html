  • Learning loop: every outcome improves the model and heuristics.

Understanding the Brief

You asked for a practical system (not a shiny demo) that respects your current dealflow and adds a dependable layer of data discipline and outreach. This design focuses on fit scoring, warm‑path discovery, and explainable insights.

Scope (Initial)

  • Entity models for Startups/Investors (stage, geo, ticket bands, sectors).
  • Ingestion pipeline + bulk tagging + manual overrides.
  • Matchmaker with adjustable weights and reasons‑why.
  • Outreach emails drafted → approve → send.

Architecture Overview

A lean, composable stack designed for clarity and speed.

  • Data: Postgres (Supabase) — entities, matches, activity.
  • Pipelines: n8n — ingest, enrich, tag, sync.
  • App: Next.js (or WeWeb) — dashboards, CRUD, insights.
  • AI: LLM for drafting outreach, insights, clustering.
  • Auth: Email/SSO; RLS policies for secure access.
